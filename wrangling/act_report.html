Analysis & Visualization Report¶

Three separate bits of analysis were carried out:

  • Ratings by dog type (Floofer, Puppo, etc)
  • Ratings by breed
  • Ratings by Time

Dog type analysis¶

Build a frame of dog type features, including the counts and mean ratings, likes and retweets, and generate plots¶

Owner ratings are similar, regardless of type¶
Puppos get most likes on average¶
Puppos get most retweets on average¶
Looking at the ratios off likes to owner ratings, we see the same pattern¶
Conclusion: The community prefers Puppos

Breed Analysis¶

Build a frame of breed features, including the counts and mean ratings, likes and retweets, and generate plots¶

Owner ratings are similar, regardless of breed¶
Shih-tzus get significantly more likes¶
Shih-tzus get significantly more retweets¶
Looking at the ratios of likes to owner ratings, we see the same pattern¶
Conclusion: The community really likes Shih-tzus, and shows a marked preference for smaller breeds generally. Combined with the preference for Puppos found above, this suggests that small dogs are cute. Who knew?

Is there a relationship between rating and timestamp?¶

Looking at the tweet frequency by hour of the day there is an obvious dip between 5 and 14 - presumably this is night-time - which makes sense if we assime that tweet times are in UTC and the user base is mainly in the USA)
Conclusion: Assuming that the period 5- 14 is when folk are asleep it is apparent that owners like their dogs early in the morning and less and less the later it gets at night.
